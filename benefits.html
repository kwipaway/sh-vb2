Benefit Certification Information

Federal Benefits (VA)

VA educational benefits are federal benefits earned by a service member or veteran. Benefit entitlement can change due to length of service, discharge characterization, and home of record/place of entry.

Learn more: VA Education Benefits

Hazlewood Benefit (State of Texas)

Veterans that entered the service with residence or place of entry as Texas receive the Hazlewood Act. This Act provides veterans with 150 credit hours to utilize in higher education.

Learn more: Texas Veterans Commission

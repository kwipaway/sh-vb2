Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to submit a “Request Certification Form” in my MySam Account each semester?

Yes. If you plan on using your VA educational benefit or Hazlewood Exemption, you must submit the semester request form in your MySam each semester.

What is a VA Certificate of Eligibility (COE)?

A Certificate of Eligibility (COE) is a determination letter from the VA sent to the student after they apply for their VA education benefit.

Do I need a new COE each semester?

No, you only submit your COE for the first semester you initiate your benefit, unless you take a long semester break (Fall or Spring).

How long will it take to process my request?

Please allow up to 20 business days for standard processing.

Will the VA pay my tuition and fees directly to SHSU?

Chapter 33 and Chapter 31: Yes.
Chapter 30, 35, and 1606: No — payment is sent directly to the student.

Can I use Hazlewood and Chapter 33 at the same time?

No (unless Chapter 33 is rated less than 100%). Chapter 31 cannot be used with Hazlewood.

Can I use my Educational Benefits for Summer Courses?

Yes, both Hazlewood and VA benefits are available for summer terms.

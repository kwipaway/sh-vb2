How to Apply for Benefits

Step 1: Get Your COE

Go to VA.gov → Education → Apply for education benefits.
Dependents: Use your own info + sponsor info.

Step 2: Submit Documents in MySam

1. Register for classes
2. Go to MySam → My Account → Financial Aid → Unsatisfied Requirements

Step 3: Contact Us

How to Apply

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Apply for your Certificate of Eligibility (COE) at VA.gov
  2. Apply and get accepted to Sam Houston State University
  3. Register for classes in MySam
  4. Submit your Request Certification Form in MySam each semester
  5. Upload all required documents through the MySam portal (DD-214, COE, Hazlewood Application, etc.)

Important Reminders

  • All documents must be submitted through the MySam portal
  • Documents sent via email, fax, or mail will not be accepted
  • Submit your request early to allow up to 20 business days for processing
  • Contact our office if you have any questions: 936-294-1440 or vetbenefits@shsu.edu

Need Help?

Our AI Chatbot is available 24/7 to answer your questions about the application process.

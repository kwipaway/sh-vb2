Available Benefits

Hazlewood Act

Texas veterans and dependents get tuition waived. Submit DD-214, Hazlewood Application, and VA COE.

Post 9/11 GI Bill (Chapter 33)

Apply for COE at VA.gov. Veterans and transferred dependents.

DEA / Chapter 35

For dependents and spouses of disabled veterans.

Chapter 31 (Veteran Readiness & Employment)

Books and supplies paid through Bearkat Bundle.

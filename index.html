Welcome Bearkats!

Sam Houston State University is committed to the success of our veteran and military-affiliated students. We provide comprehensive assistance in navigating GI Bill benefits, coordinating with campus administrative offices, and accessing specialized academic advising. Our goal is to streamline your transition to campus life by connecting you with the specific resources and community networks necessary for your success.

What We Offer

  • Fully online document submission system
  • Step-by-step guidance on benefit utilization
  • Clear answers to your questions about VA and TVC education benefits
  • Timely updates and support to ensure you meet deadlines and requirements

Important Note

All documents must be submitted through our online certification request system in your MySam account. Documents submitted via email, fax, or mail will not be accepted.
